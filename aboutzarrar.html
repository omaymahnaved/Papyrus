Good communication is a leadership attribute for a number of reasons.A good leader understands that a level of creativity is needed for effective problem solving.Taking risks and making brave decisions. Brave: Facing challenges and difficulties with courage. A team leader provides guidance and instruction to a working group about a project or portfolio of projects. They are in charge of delegating work, overseeing progress towards goals, and coaching team members as needed. Team leads often serve as de-facto mentors for the team, even if they don't have a manager title. A team leader has an overview of a group of people, motivates, gives instruction and monitors performance.
